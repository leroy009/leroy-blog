## How to Make Fluffy Pancakes

A reliable batter and cook method for fluffy pancakes.

### Ingredients

- 1 cup flour, 1 tbsp sugar, 1 tsp baking powder, pinch of salt
- 1 cup milk, 1 egg, 2 tbsp melted butter

### Steps

1. Whisk dry ingredients.
2. Mix wet ingredients then combine without overmixing.
3. Cook on medium heat until bubbles form; flip and finish.
