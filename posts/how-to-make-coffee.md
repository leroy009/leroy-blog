## How to Make Great Coffee

A dependable method for a tasty cup using a drip brewer or French press.

### Ingredients & Tools

- Fresh coffee beans
- Grinder (burr recommended)
- Scale or measuring spoon
- Hot water (195–205°F / 90–96°C)

### Method (Pour-over / Drip)

1. Grind beans to medium-fine.
2. Use 1:15–1:17 coffee-to-water ratio (e.g., 20g coffee : 300g water).
3. Wet the filter and add grounds.
4. Pour water slowly in concentric circles.
5. Let brew ~2.5–3.5 minutes.

### French Press Variation

- Coarser grind, steep 4 minutes, then plunge slowly.

Enjoy immediately for best flavor.
