## How to Make a Sourdough Starter

A 5–7 day process to cultivate natural yeast for sourdough baking.

### Ingredients

- Whole wheat or rye flour
- Unchlorinated water

### Day-by-day

1. Day 1: Mix 50g flour + 50g water in a jar. Cover loosely.
2. Day 2–5: Each day discard half and feed with 50g flour + 50g water.
3. Around day 5–7: Starter should double in 4–6 hours and be bubbly.

### Tips

- Keep at room temperature for faster activity.
- Store refrigerated and feed weekly if not used.
