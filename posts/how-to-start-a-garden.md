## How to Start a Small Garden

Plan, prepare soil, and choose easy plants.

### Steps

1. Pick a sunny spot (6+ hours of sun).
2. Prepare soil: loosen and enrich with compost.
3. Choose easy plants: lettuce, basil, radishes, tomatoes.
4. Water regularly and mulch to retain moisture.

### Tips

- Start with containers if space is limited.
- Rotate crops each season for soil health.
