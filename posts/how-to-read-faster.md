## How to Read a Book Faster

Improve speed without sacrificing comprehension.

### Techniques

- Preview the table of contents and chapter headings.
- Set a purpose for reading (skimming vs deep reading).
- Use a pointer (finger or pen) to keep your eyes moving.

### Tips

- Practice with non-fiction first.
- Take short, spaced breaks to maintain focus.
