## How to Change a Flat Tire

Stay safe and prepared with these clear steps.

### Safety First

- Move to a flat, safe location away from traffic.
- Turn on hazard lights and apply parking brake.

### Steps

1. Loosen lug nuts slightly while the car is still on the ground.
2. Jack up the vehicle following the owner's manual lift point.
3. Remove lug nuts and the flat tire.
4. Mount the spare tire and hand-tighten lug nuts.
5. Lower the vehicle and tighten lug nuts in a star pattern.

### Tips

- Check spare tire pressure regularly.
- Replace temporary spares as soon as possible.
